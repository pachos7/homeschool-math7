Math 7th Grade Curriculum

A comprehensive 36-week program

Welcome

This curriculum is designed to provide a thorough foundation in mathematics and related sciences for 7th grade students. The program is divided into five key parts, each focusing on essential skills and concepts.

Curriculum Parts

Part I: Remembering Math Skills

Review and reinforce fundamental mathematical concepts and operations.

Part II: Global Economics and Investment

Explore economic principles, financial literacy, and investment concepts.

Part III: Algebra and Geometry Foundations

Build essential algebraic thinking and geometric reasoning skills.

Part IV: Mathematical Change and Function Concepts

Understand patterns, functions, and mathematical relationships.

Part V: Introduction to Physical and Chemical Science

Apply mathematical thinking to physical and chemical science concepts.
