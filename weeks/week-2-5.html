Week 2.5: Currency Exchange Simulation

← Back to Part II: Global Economics and Investment

Objective

  • Understand what currency exchange is and how it works.
  • Practice converting between different currencies (COP, USD, EUR).
  • Analyze how exchange rates vary over time.
  • Compare exchange rates across different vendors and platforms.
  • Explore purchasing power parity through the Big Mac Index.

Tools

  • Calculator (physical or digital)
  • Google Finance - Current Exchange Rates
  • Exchange Rate Comparison Sites:
    • Western Union Currency Converter (USD to COP)
    • Dolar Hoy - Casas de Cambio
  • Spreadsheet software for tracking and comparing conversions

Content

🎥 Introduction to Currency and Exchange

Watch these videos to understand currency and currency exchange. Take notes and discuss your observations:

  • What is Currency? - Educational Video
  • Understanding Currency Exchange - Educational Video

Discussion Points: What did you notice? How do exchange rates work? Why do they change?

💱 Activity 1: Convert Currency with Today's Rates

Starting Amount: 2,000,000 COP

Using the Google Finance link (or other currency converters), convert the amount:

  • From COP to USD
  • From COP to EUR
  • From USD back to COP
  • From EUR back to COP

Questions to explore:

  • What is the current exchange rate for each conversion?
  • If you convert COP → USD → COP, do you get the same amount back? Why or why not?

📅 Activity 2: Exchange Rates Over Time

Starting Amount: 2,000,000 COP

Compare exchange rates from one year ago vs. today:

  • Convert 2,000,000 COP to USD using last year's rate
  • Convert 2,000,000 COP to EUR using last year's rate
  • Convert using today's rates

Analysis:

  • Create a comparison table showing the difference
  • Which currency has changed more against COP over the year?
  • If you had converted your money a year ago, would you have more or less value today?

� Activity 3: Exchange Rates Across Vendors

Compare exchange rates for the same conversion across different platforms:

  • Use Western Union's converter
  • Use www.dolarhoy.co
  • Use Google Finance

Questions:

  • Are the rates the same across all vendors?
  • If not, why might they differ?
  • Which vendor offers the best rate for converting USD to COP?
  • What fees or commissions might affect the actual amount you receive?

💡 Real-World Connection

Understanding currency exchange helps you:

  • Make smart decisions when traveling internationally
  • Understand global economics and trade
  • Compare purchasing power across different countries
  • Save money by choosing the best exchange rates

Challenges

  • Complete all three currency conversion activities (today's rates, time comparison, vendor comparison)
  • Create a detailed table comparing exchange rates from one year ago vs. today for COP → USD and COP → EUR
  • Calculate the percentage change in exchange rates over the past year
  • Create a vendor comparison chart showing which platform offers the best rates
  • Big Mac Index Challenge: Review the Big Mac Index by Country. Note and discuss your observations:
    • How much does a Big Mac cost in Colombia vs. the US vs. a European country?
    • What does this tell you about purchasing power in different countries?
    • How does the Big Mac Index relate to currency exchange rates?
  • Write a one-paragraph reflection: Why do you think exchange rates change over time?
