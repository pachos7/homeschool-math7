Objective

  • Understand what GDP (Gross Domestic Product) is and how it's calculated.
  • Learn about inflation and its effect on purchasing power.
  • Explore the basics of global trade and international economics.
  • Apply mathematical concepts to analyze economic indicators.

Tools

  • US Inflation Calculator

Content

📚 Key Topics

  • What is GDP and why does it matter?
  • Understanding inflation: causes and effects
  • Global trade: imports, exports, and trade balance
  • How these economic indicators interconnect

💡 Real-World Connection

Understanding these economic concepts helps you:

  • Make informed decisions about savings and spending
  • Understand news about the economy
  • See how local and global economies are connected
  • Prepare for financial planning in your future

Challenges

  • Compare the GDP of 5 different countries and create a bar graph
  • Calculate how much a $50 item from 10 years ago would cost today using an inflation calculator
  • Research: What are the top 3 exports and imports for your country?
  • Discussion: How does inflation affect your family's budget?
