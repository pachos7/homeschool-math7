💡 Key Concepts

Balancing Equations:

  • What you do to one side of the equation, you must do to the other side
  • The goal is to isolate the variable (get x by itself)
  • Use inverse operations: addition undoes subtraction, and vice versa

Examples:

  • If x + 5 = 12, subtract 5 from both sides → x = 7
  • If x - 3 = 10, add 3 to both sides → x = 13
